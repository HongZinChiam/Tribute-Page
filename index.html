Homelander

Leader of the Seven

In our world, we are truly blessed to have Homelander, the ultimate symbol of heroism and patriotism. As the charismatic leader of The Seven, our most beloved superhero team, he embodies the very essence of American ideals and values. He has saved countless lives and thwarted numerous threats to our national security. His charming demeanor and unwavering dedication to justice make him a beacon of hope and a protector of the innocent. Whether soaring through the skies or standing tall at public events, Homelander inspires admiration and trust, representing the best of what humanity can aspire to be. Under the aegis of Vought International, he serves not just as a hero but as a living testament to the power of courage, integrity, and unwavering resolve in the face of adversity.
